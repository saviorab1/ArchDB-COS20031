Round Definitions

Standard archery rounds and their specifications

WA 1440

Distance: 90m, 70m, 50m, 30m
Ends: 6 per distance (24 total)
Max Score: 1440 points
Arrows: 144 arrows
Type: Outdoor Competition

Ends: 20 ends
